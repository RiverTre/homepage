 Hi!

I graduated with a Master degree in Data Science at Wuhan University advised by Prof. Wei Lu. I graduated from WHU with a B.S. in Library Science. I love studying and learning. I am happy to mentor undergraduate or fresh master students interested in research. I am working at PCL as a machine learning engineer focusing on training LLMs.
Currently I am open to Ph.D. position opportunities : )

 Research Interests

My main research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning. I am particularly interested in scientific document processing, LMs beneficial to society and AI for good. My goal is to build LMs with less potential to be misused and to help tackling the problems exacerbated by LLMs such as misinformation.
